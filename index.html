Strategic Opportunity — One Domain, One Front Door

TAC’s search equity is currently split across three versions of the same homepage: http://ta-concepts.com, https://ta-concepts.com, and https://www.ta-concepts.com. The crawl confirmed the HTTP variant returns a live page with no canonical tag and no 301 redirect — and TAC’s single most valuable ranking, #1 for “building automation controls companies” (69% of all organic traffic), resolves through the www variant with a UTM tracking tag appended. Google is dividing what little authority the domain has (Authority Score 6) across competing URLs. A rebuild consolidates everything to one canonical domain with clean 301s, so every backlink, citation, and ranking reinforces the same front door.
